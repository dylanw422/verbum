## 1. AI-Powered Intelligence Layer

### 1.1 Personal AI Scripture Companion
- **Conversational AI** assistant trained on biblical scholarship, commentaries, and cross-references
- Ask questions like *"What does Paul mean by 'grace' in Ephesians 2?"* and get scholarly, multi-perspective answers
- Voice-enabled for hands-free devotional time
- Context-aware suggestions based on what you just read

### 1.2 AI-Generated Study Plans
- **Adaptive learning paths** that adjust based on reading patterns, comprehension signals (pause frequency, re-reads), and declared goals
- Generate custom reading plans for life situations: grief, anxiety, new job, marriage, parenting
- Spaced repetition system (SRS) for memorization with AI-optimized review intervals

### 1.3 Semantic Search & Discovery
- **Natural language search** across all translations: *"verses about overcoming fear"*
- Cross-reference engine showing thematic connections across Old and New Testament
- **Concept mapping** visualization showing how themes connect across books

### 1.4 Real-time Translation & Original Language
- One-tap access to Greek/Hebrew with inline interlinear display
- AI pronunciation guide with audio for original languages
- **Word study deep-dives** showing etymology, usage across scripture, and scholarly notes

## 3. Advanced RSVP & Reading Engine

### 3.1 Biometric Integration
- **Eye tracking** (via webcam or Apple Vision Pro) to auto-pause when attention drifts
- Heart rate variability integration for optimal reading pace
- Adjust speed based on cognitive load signals

### 3.2 Comprehension Testing
- Optional micro-quizzes after chapters to reinforce retention
- **Active recall prompts** integrated into reading flow
- AI-generated comprehension questions

### 3.3 Multi-Modal Reading
- **Audio + Visual** synchronized mode: hear the word as you see it (TTS)
- **Dynamic typography**: Words animate with emphasis (bold/italic) based on meaning
- **Graphic visualizations** for geographic, genealogical, or chronological passages

### 3.4 Distraction-Free Modes
- **Focus Sessions** with Pomodoro-style timers
- Screen time integration to surface Verbum as a mindful alternative
- Wind-down mode that transitions to calming ambient sounds
